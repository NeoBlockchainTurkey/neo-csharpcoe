## What is the NEO Enterprise Application Competition #1?

Co-sponsored by the [NEO Blockchain C# Center of Excellence](https://github.com/mwherman2000/neo-csharpcoe/blob/master/README.md) ([neo-csharpcoe](https://github.com/mwherman2000/neo-csharpcoe/blob/master/README.md)), this contest is an unparalleled opportunity to focus your creativity, passion, and ambition on enterprise applications of the NEO Blockchain.

## What is the [NEO Blockchain C# Center of Excellence](https://github.com/mwherman2000/neo-csharpcoe/blob/master/README.md)?

The `neo-csharpcoe` project is an "umbrella" project for several initiatives related to providing tools and libraries (code), frameworks, how-to documentation, and best practices for enterprise application development using .NET/C#, C#.NEO and the NEO Blockchain software platform.

The `neo-csharpcoe` is an independent, free, open source project that is 100% community-supported by people like yourself through your contributions of time, energy, passion, promotion, and donations.
